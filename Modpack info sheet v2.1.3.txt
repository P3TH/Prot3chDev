Name: prot3ch private server modpack (Open to whitelisting but the code is completely private and given out individually).
Version: 1.4.7
Version: 1.2.3

Description:
This modpack is made for the members of a server called prot3ch,
it includes mods that no other modpack includes. 
Lockit, atmosmobs and millanaire are very rare to come by
in a modpack. We update our modpack and have a bug report
section for anyone that needs help.
https://github.com/profjb/Prot3ch/issues?labels=&page=1&state=open
Other than that, check the site if you run on mac/linux.
On windows your free to go!
